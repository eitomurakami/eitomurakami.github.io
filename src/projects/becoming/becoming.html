Becoming

Becoming is an operatic virtual reality experience based on a Persian poem by Mowlana Rumi. While the piece is a major artistic endeavor, it also showcases a number of key technologies and streaming techniques for the development of musical content which depends on high-quality audio.
As a graphic and sound designer, I develop audiovisual infrastructure for a virtual reality world using Unreal Engine under the the supervision of professor Shahrokh Yadegari.

More information about this project can be found on the Sonic Arts website.
