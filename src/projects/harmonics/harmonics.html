Pd Integer Harmonic Generator

Pd additive synthesizer with parameters to dynamically change the frequency and amplitude of each integer harmonic.

The harmonics are odd multiples of the fundamental frequency by default. The transposition value with a range of 0 to 1 shifts the default odd ratio to the even ratio or non-integer ratios in between. For each harmonic, the transposition value is multiplied by a value at the corresponding index of the "mult" array. The amplitude of each harmonic is defined as a reciprocal of its harmonic number. The amplitude of each harmonic is then multiplied by a value at the corresponding index of the "amp" array.
