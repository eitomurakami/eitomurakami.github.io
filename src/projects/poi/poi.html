Pigments Of Imagination (2021)

Pigments of Imagination (2021) is a collaborative audiovisual project by Gabriel Zalles Ballivian, Tim Gmeiner, and Eito Murakami. The main element of the project is a popular music track touching on themes of imagination and space travel. The song is composed and produced by Timothy “Ill Poetic” Gmeiner, and features vocals by Ill Poetic and Nick Tolford, flugelhorn by renowned jazz artist Stephanie Richards, and additional synthesizer parts by DJ/Producer King Britt.

The A-frame project offers a dynamic virtual reality experience that accompanies the song. Several spheres were textured and set to rotate around the listener, serving the theme of outer space. A 360 degree photograph from the European Southern Observatory was used to texture the sky element. Additionally, some GLTF objects collected from Google Poly were imported into the scene.
