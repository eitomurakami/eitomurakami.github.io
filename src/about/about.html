About

Eito Murakami is an undergraduate student at University of California San Diego double majoring in Political Science/International Relations and Interdisciplinary Computing and the Arts (ICAM) Music. He is an electronic musician, sound designer, instrument designer, and software engineer. By combining his classical music training with coding proficiency, he creates software and instruments that promote intuitive musical performance. Murakami is currently a member of the Sonic Arts research team as a graphic and sound designer. Under the supervision of professor Shahrokh Yadegari, he develops audiovisual infrastructure for a virtual reality world using the Unreal Engine. Additionally, he is an instructor and marketing/production assistant for The Deejays & Vinylphiles Club (DVC) at UCSD.
